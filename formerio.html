About Me

Edward Cheever is a full stack Web Developer currently completing a rigorous coding Bootcamp at Northwestern University in Evanston, IL, gaining extensive skills in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Node.js, as well as JavaScript libraries like JQuery and React.
Before that, he spent five years as an accredited and accomplished full and part time teacher of middle and high school students in the areas of technology, english/language arts, history, social science, and geography.
Alongside his teaching certification, he has a B.A. in English, and a B.S. in Social Science from Southwestern Adventist University in Keene, TX. He received several University Writing and tutoring awards, and is a founding member of the student organization, the Rough Writers.
In everything he does, Edward brings a strong sense of determination and responsibility to see a task through from beginning to end, and to do so with quality and creativity.
